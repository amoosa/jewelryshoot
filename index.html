Let's get in touch...

We do freelance jewelry photography for ecommerce sites - sell via your own store or via a marketplace like ebay, etsy, amazon and others. We charge $10/image for web-ready product photos on a white background. Plus for every 50 products, we give you two FREE group shots. A group shot is a photograph of 3-5 items together than you can use on your homepage, facebook page, emails, etc.

Please reach out at shoot24x7@gmail.com with any questions or if you're ready to move ahead, please send me your project details including number of items, timeline and any special instructions so we can get your job scheduled.

How it works

We're in the San Francisco Bay Area - Berkeley, CA 94702. After we finalize details, ship us your items and we'll ship them back to you after we complete the project.
